OUR STORY

From a Small Workshop
to Varanasi's Go-To Repair Service

ElectroPro was founded in 2016 with a simple mission: provide fast, honest, and affordable electronics repair for Varanasi households. What started with TV and AC repairs in Lanka quickly grew as word spread about our quality and transparency across Varanasi.

Today, we serve all major areas of Varanasi with a team of 8 certified technicians, handling everything from basic appliance fixes to complex circuit board microsoldering.

  • 8+ years of electronics repair experience
  • Certified and background-verified technicians
  • All major brands and models covered
  • Genuine spare parts used — always
  • 90-day service warranty on every job
  • 5000+ repair jobs completed
